Why Gold is Timeless

Gold has been cherished for thousands of years as a symbol of wealth, purity, and prestige. From ancient civilizations to modern jewellery houses, gold remains the most celebrated metal in fine jewellery.

At Payal & Minal Gems, we use the finest gold alloys to create jewellery that blends tradition with innovation.

Types of Gold by Purity

24K Gold

99.9% pure gold, soft and not usually used for jewellery, but valued for investment.

22K Gold

91.6% pure, durable, and most common for traditional Indian jewellery.

18K Gold

75% pure, stronger and ideal for diamond and gemstone-studded jewellery.

Gold Colours

Pure gold is naturally yellow, but by mixing with other metals, jewellers create a palette of colors: - Yellow Gold: Classic and traditional. - White Gold: Alloyed with palladium/nickel, modern and sleek. - Rose Gold: Mixed with copper for a romantic blush. - Green Gold: Rare alloy with silver, offering a unique shade.
