Diamond Cutting Process

The transformation of a rough diamond into a sparkling gem involves precision, artistry, and technology. Below is the step-by-step process:

1. Planning

Experts analyze the rough diamond to determine the best cut, maximizing brilliance and minimizing waste.

2. Cleaving or Sawing

The rough diamond is split or sawn into smaller, workable pieces using advanced laser technology.

3. Bruting

In this step, the diamond is shaped into a round outline by grinding against another diamond.

4. Polishing

Facets are carefully cut and polished, bringing out the brilliance and fire of the diamond.

5. Inspection

Each diamond undergoes rigorous quality control to ensure it meets international standards of clarity and cut.
