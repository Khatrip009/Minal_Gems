Jewellery Making Process

From imagination to timeless creation

A Journey of Craftsmanship

Every piece of jewellery is a masterpiece born from a blend of art, skill, and technology. At Payal & Minal Gems, we ensure that each ornament goes through a meticulous process to deliver unmatched quality and beauty.

1.

Designing

The process begins with an idea. Our designers create sketches and 3D CAD models to visualize the jewellery piece. Every curve, angle, and gem placement is planned with precision.

2.

Wax Model / Prototype

A wax or resin prototype is created using 3D printing or traditional carving. This model acts as the base for casting the jewellery piece.

3.

Casting

The wax model is encased in plaster and melted away, leaving a cavity. Molten gold is poured into this mold, forming the raw structure of the jewellery.

4.

Stone Setting

Skilled artisans carefully set diamonds and gemstones into the jewellery, ensuring perfect alignment, security, and brilliance.

5.

Polishing & Finishing

The raw piece is polished, plated (if needed), and finished to perfection. This stage enhances the shine and ensures smoothness for comfort.
